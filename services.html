Our Services

Taxis

Our Taxi Services are available to you 24 hours a day everyday. Our taxis are also eco-friendly. We don't charge you by the mile! We use a flat rate for all your metro travel needs. Certain restrictions apply, but they're not big ones!

Our drivers know the most direct routes to your destination. They also know the best scenic routes. You decide!

Shuttles

Are you traveling with a large group who are all going to the same destination? Our shuttle service is just perfect for that! Each shuttle can accomodate 10 passengers and their luggage!

Shuttles are also a convenient way to share expenses with other people. When you split the cost with other passengers, you save lots of money! Spend it at Disney World instead!

Limousines

Special occasions call for a special vehicle. Our luxurious limousines will turn heads where you go. They are ultra plush and equipped with a soda and liquor bar. Do you want to wath a little TV between destinations? You can! Want to check your email? You can!

We're not responsible if someone mistakes you for a Rock Star, though!

Rentals

Sometimes you might just want to be a do-it-yourselfer. If you don't to just see the sights, but be a part of the sights, a scooter might be just what you're looking for? Not in hurry? How about a state-of-the-art bicycle?

The Orlando metro area has many bike paths that you'll love. You can drink in the scenery at your leisure!
